Avocado Toast

Vegan Avocado Toast

This is a delicious, healthy, hearty breakfast recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 avocado, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Directions

  1. Toast bread in a toaster or toaster oven.

  2. Scoop avocado into a bowl. Add parsley, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder;
    mash together using a potato masher. Spread avocado mixture into each piece of toast.
